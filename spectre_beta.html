Duel Booting Windows 10 and Arch Linux - - on the HP Spectre

I got back into chroot from the usb drive to install dialog, iw, and wpa_supplicant because i forgot to before and the internet was being sketchy i also forgot to make a user and add me to the sudoers file pgp keys were messed up, fixed it but it was hanging so i had to bang on the keyboard didnt reboot at first, gave an error, probably a result of fogetting sudo was getting a device warning on boot, fixed by changing 3 instances of ro to rw in grub.cfg getting another device warning at boot, but the internet says it is harmless, so... X wouldn't start, turned out I installed the wrong drivers (I'm lame) But after all that it works!
