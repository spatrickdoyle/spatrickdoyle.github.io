Cut a couple preliminary tests. Cut a whole bunch with different materials and laser powers. Tested out with masking tape coating. What I learned: Acrylic looks daaaaaaaaaamn good. Everything looks WAAAAAAAAY better with a coating. Aim low when setting the laser, otherwise it can cut all the way through the material. Raster makes it way easier to peel off the coating. Masking tape works pretty well when the material doesn't have a coating already.
